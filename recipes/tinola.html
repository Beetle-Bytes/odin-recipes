Tinola

Description

Tinola is an easy, one-pot soup from the Philippines. Chicken, chayote squash (or papaya!), bok choy, and spinach are simmered with fresh ginger in a savory broth that's often served with white rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 green papaya, wedged
  • 1 ½ cup malunggay leaves
  • 1 cup hot pepper leaves
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed and chopped
  • 1 onions, chopped
  • 3 thumbs ginger, julienned
  • 2 Maggi Magic Chicken Cubes
  • 1 quart rice wash
  • 1 quart water, as needed
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Ground black pepper to taste (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Steps

  1. Sauté the aromatics
  2. brown the chicken
  3. add seasoning and rice wash
  4. cook the green papaya
  5. add greens, season, and serve!
